The cow is a four-footed domestic animal. It is found everywhere in the world. It is very useful to man. The cow has four legs, two eyes, two horns and a long tail with a tuft of hair at the end. Its body is covered with hair. It has some teeth in her lower jaw. It has no teeth in the upper jaw. There are many kinds of the cow. Some are large and some are small in size. Some are white, some are red, some are black and some are of mixed color. The cow is found in all parts of the world.

Wild cows are also found in the forest. The cow lives on grass and straw. She also eats oil-cake, rice and bran. The cow is a very gentle creature. The cow s very useful animal. From her milk. We got curd, butter, ghee and many other things. The cow plows our field. cow dung is a good manure. Shoes, bags, belts are made of its skin. The cow is very useful to us. So, we should take care of her.
